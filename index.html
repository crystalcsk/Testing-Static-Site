A Unique Collection

These lists of creature names were compiled over decades by the late Cherokee elder Hastings Shade. Beginning in the 1970s, this unique archive was created by Hastings through consultation with Cherokee elders and knowledge keepers.

These lists do not encompass all creature names in the Cherokee language, as many common creature names are known in Cherokee community and listed in Cherokee dictionaries. However, these lists do represent the largest archive of Cherokee creature names in existence and include many creature names that are documented here for the first time.

These lists are part of a larger publication, The Cherokee Natural World. If you are new to this publication and interested in Cherokee creature names, we encourage you to first visit The Cherokee Natural World as it explains the importance of names and naming in Cherokee culture, as well as how this archive of creature names was collected.
